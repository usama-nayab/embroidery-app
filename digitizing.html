    Regular/Full Stitch Digitizing

    3D PUFF

    3D puff embroidery uses an underlying foam material, which raises a design up allowing for the three dimensional feel and appearance, one can not use the regular stitch designs for 3D they need to be created accordingly.

    APPLIQUE

    AppliquÃ© is ornamental needlework in which pieces or patches of fabric in different shapes and patterns are sewn or stuck onto a larger piece to form a picture or pattern. It is commonly used as decoration, especially on garments. The technique is accomplished either by hand stitching or machine; this technique is also used to reduce stitch count of a design.

    TACKLE TWILL APPLIQUE

    Tackle Twill Applique is basically a branch of applique which is created with run and zig zag stitches with no smooth satin covering on top like on borders like regular applique Tackle twill is cut pieces of fabric that are sewn onto a apparel like sweatshirts, jackets and athletic jerseys.. Any shape, letter, or design can be custom cut from a polyester twill material and sewn onto almost any garment for an extremely finished look.

    FELT APPLIQUE

    Felt Applique is another branch of applique the only difference is that the cut pieces or fabric are attached to garment with the help of run stitches only without an zig zag or satin covering on top of it this is the msot rare applique style used by commercial embrodiers

    Cross stitching can be done with any embroidery machine. This approach uses lay down the stitches in a pattern to attain a picture-like glance or hand made look. Cross stitch is a great choice for various embroidery applications, like quilting and home furnishing etc
